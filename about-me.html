This is my history

Like many of you, I am an immigrant. I arrived in the USA in December 1983, with my husband and my five-year-old daughter; Within a few months, my youngest daughter was born prematurely.

From that moment, I had access to the US Health System due to the delicate conditions with which my daughter was born, her weight was 1 pound 6 ounces, which led to a series of surgeries, endless treatments and 6 months in two different hospitals that generated expenses that were impossible to cover.Â I feel blessed because she is a very active part of our family. This knowledge in the Health System is vast thanks to that personal experience. I have personally gone through all the stages of health coverage, from group coverage, individual coverage, Medicaid and at the same time, covering all the regulatory aspects of Social Security.

My personal expirience

Later, my niece at the age of 15 presented a disease of the digestive system called Crohn's, there were three years of permanent treatments, hospitalisations, transfusions and other medical services. My sister and brother-in-law had excellent health coverage that allowed them to give my niece the necessary medical care and emotional support. With deep joy I can affirm that despite such a delicate illness, she got ahead and currently works as a professional.

Finally, I share with you one of the most complicated experiences that my family and I have lived; It was my mother's illness and not having Health Coverage. I made sure that she always received the necessary medical care and services; However, as I was legally responsible, the hospital admissions caused a serious financial situation, six months of care cost around $ 200,000.
All these experiences created in me a desire to help the community to understand the Health System and provide them with options that fit their needs and budget.If you need help or have questions, click and we will gladly help free of charge.
